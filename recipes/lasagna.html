Lasagna

Honestly I don't know how to make tacos

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Marinara Sauce
  • Beef

Steps

  1. Apply a layer of lasagna to the bottom of your pan
  2. Apply a layer of cheese
  3. Apply a layer of sauce
  4. Apply a layer of ground beef
  5. Apply a new layer of pasta onto previous layers
  6. repeat until your pan is full, make sure a pasta layer is on the top and cover with cheese
  7. Bake then serve!
