Tacos

Tacos are pretty yummy

Ingredients

  • Tortillas
  • Meat of your choice
  • Salsa of your choice
  • Cheese
  • Cilantro

Steps

  1. Heat up tortillas on stovetop
  2. Melt cheese on warm tortillas
  3. Put your meat and cilantro on tortillas
  4. Apply Salsa and or limes onto tacos
  5. You now have tacos!
